Map of Previous Living out Locations

(Update: With the new Dorothy Wadham Building up now, this is now an artefact showing the struggle of students from a time when dear old Wadham still couldn’t afford to house all its students. Be grateful for what you have now kids)

Here’s a map showing where ex-Wadham 2nd years have lived out in previous years. This comes after feedback I got that the house search for freshers for their 2nd year would be easier if they could have a feel for where people tend to live when living out, and what the previous Wadham students have felt about their houses. Feel free to message me if you have any questions/suggestions for improvements!
