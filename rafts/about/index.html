About Me

A Brief Description About Me and My Experiences

Welcome!

This website is an attempt to catalogue my many experiences and projects over the past few years. I have worked in a few software companies, an investment bank, the army, and I have served and represented fellow students in our Student's Union in various capacities, all the while meeting new and interesting people, doing interesting projects and in general, having a pretty good time!

Do stay tuned as I keep updating this site with more new and old projects and experiences.
